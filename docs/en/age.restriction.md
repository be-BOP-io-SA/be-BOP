# Age Restriction

![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2e8ff422-35d9-4a39-a285-24c0000616b3)

Available in **Admin** > **Config** > **Age restriction**, this section allows you to configure an age restriction for your be-BOP, according to legal requirements. Use this feature to limit access to your products or services based on user age.

## Configuration Settings

- [] **My online business needs age limitation & validation**: Check this box if your business requires age limitation and validation.
- [] **Legal reason**: Indicate the legal reason for the age restriction. For example, mention specific laws or regulations that justify this restriction.

  ![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e0d5b7dd-6340-4a49-b0d9-a2957ced6fca)

## Restriction Page Configuration

Available at the suggested CMS pages level, the agewall page is a CMS page whose content will be embedded in the age restriction form.

## Restriction Page Display

If the age restriction is added to your be-BOP, users will see a restriction form that prevents them from accessing the site until they accept the age restrictions.
