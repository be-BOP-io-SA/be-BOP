# Challenge Widget Documentation

Accessible via **Admin** > **Widgets** > **Challenge**, challenge widgets can be used in your be-BOP to track challenges on sales, support, etc. They can be integrated into CMS zones or pages.

![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/88bfde44-acee-40e9-8b81-4bb278072065)

## Adding a Challenge

To add a challenge, click on **Add challenge**.

![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5797b4ca-f7d7-4fa0-9f5e-3fe7e4349083)

### 1. **Challenge name**

The challenge name. A short, descriptive title that summarizes the challenge objective.

### 2. **Mode**

The challenge mode, which can be a number of items sold (**TotalProducts**) or an amount of money to collect (**MoneyAmount**).

### 3. **Goal**

The target to reach for the challenge. Accompanied by a **currency** field if the mode is **MoneyAmount**, representing the currency.

### 4. **Beginning date**

The date the challenge starts.

### 5. **Ending date**

The deadline to complete the challenge.

### 6. **Products**

A list of products whose sales contribute to the challenge progress.

## CMS Integration

To integrate a challenge into a CMS zone or page, add it as follows: `[Challenge=slug]`.
