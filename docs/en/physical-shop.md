# Shop Information

This section (in **Admin** > **Config** > **Physical Shop**) allows you to configure the legal information, address, contact details, and banking information of your shop. This information will appear on invoices and official documents.

## Legal Information

- **Business Name**: Official company name (example: Agiltoo).
- **VAT Number**: VAT identification number.

  ![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/be9d75f2-2a5c-47df-93f8-cba04ef14f6b)

## Company Address

- **Street**: Address or street where the company is located (example: Ville neuve d'ascq).
- **Country**: Country where the company is located (example: France).
- **State**: Region or department.
- **City**: City where the company is located (example: Lille).
- **Zip code**: Company city postal code (example: 59000).

  ![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/de72af00-58aa-42c9-971a-3b1729653bf2)

## Contact Information

- **Email**: Company contact email address (example: ithiame@agiltoo.fr).
- **Phone**: Contact phone number (example: 776023135).

  ![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/879942cb-3742-4701-adb1-a78464307f06)

## Bank Account

- **IBAN**: Company bank account IBAN number (example: FR7630001007941234567890185).
- **BIC**: Bank account BIC/SWIFT code (example: 30001).

  ![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/37606db8-ce77-40ae-9dc3-5ce90028dfc1)

## Invoice Information

- **Very-top-right issuer information**: Optional field to add information that will appear in the top-right corner of the invoice.

Click **Update** to save all entered information. This information will be used for issuing invoices and displaying your shop's contact details.

![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f88df099-6673-4ff1-a6db-b640114b9504)

## Display Seller Identity in the Footer

To do this, go to **Admin** > **Merch** > **Layout** and in the **footer** section, check [x] `Display main shop informations` and click update to save.

![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8f94f866-92e0-4389-9d0c-7fc2172e1da3)

And you will now have your main shop information displayed in your footer.

![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/3a70b8ad-99cb-4968-bb9d-27c1cd7cd05f)
