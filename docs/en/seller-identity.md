# Seller Identity

This section (in **Admin** > **Config** > **Seller Identity**) allows you to configure the legal and contact information of your company to appear on invoices and official documents.

## Legal Information

- **Business name**: Official company name.
- **VAT Number**: Company VAT identification number.

  ![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/79a212e0-1cd3-4685-a4d0-317a87be08d1)

## Company Address

- **Street**: Address or street where the company is located.
- **Country**: Select the country where the company is located.
- **State**: Region or department (if applicable).
- **City**: City where the company is located.
- **Zip Code**: Company city postal code.

  ![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7c34cc61-8ce9-4316-8ca0-cbc4913fc24f)

## Contact Information

- **Email**: Company contact email address.
- **Phone**: Contact phone number.

  ![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/7e4c7d60-e8b8-4dbb-a63c-85ef3fd6f0d5)

## Bank Account

- **Account Holder Name**: Name of the bank account holder.
- **Account Holder Address**: Address of the account holder.
- **IBAN**: Company bank account IBAN number.
- **BIC**: Bank account BIC/SWIFT code.

  ![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5b7ba17b-6607-4f6f-9998-8f49bc8948fd)

After filling in your banking information, your be-BOP will have a new payment method: **Bank Transfer**.

![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f0baa919-86aa-432b-a10f-b26b83028e0d)

## Invoice Information

- **Fill with main shop informations**: Button to automatically fill the billing information with your main shop information.
- **Very-top-right issuer information**: Optional text that will appear in the top-right corner of the invoice.

Click **Update** to save all entered information. This information will be used for issuing invoices and displaying your company's contact details.

![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d222402e-f2be-41d2-95a2-c6f16a6c14e0)

## Display Seller Identity in the Footer

To do this, go to **Admin** > **Merch** > **Layout** and in the **footer** section, check [x] `Display company identity & company contact` and click update to save.

![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f3adba2c-262b-42d1-951e-7a250343f2c4)

And you will now have the company information displayed in your footer.

![image](https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/624bf24d-88fd-4d3c-b14f-77872dd56b1c)
